Fratello Pizzas Logo

The original Fratello's logo featured a semi-realistic looking an actual pizza, and the Fratello's Pizza wordmark usually being placed to either side of the pizza.
Currently The triangles on the logo represent the brotherhood and the family. Originally, Fratello's had planned to add a triangle for each new restaurant opened.

A quick look to our Story...

What Fratello Pizzas is today began in 2020 with the dream of two local brothers of San Cristobal city, both with a big sensation of love for cooking,
with not much money in their pockets but with a big motivation and their family support.

2020

They sell their first 4 pizzas for a group of friends in a meeting, all people loved those pizzas and the voice starts spread on the city. They start only doing delivery because of the quarantine.

2021

Fratellos's brothers open their first local store and it was an amazing success for them. They started offering jobs for the locals and people of the city keeps in love with their pizzas
